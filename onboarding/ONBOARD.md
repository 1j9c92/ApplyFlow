# ApplyFlow Onboarding

Welcome to ApplyFlow — a self-improving job application pipeline that uses Claude to automate and optimize your search.

## What ApplyFlow Does

ApplyFlow works in a loop:

1. **Find** jobs matching your criteria (from boards, your network, company career pages)
2. **Tailor** your resume and cover letter to each opportunity using your personalized career context
3. **Apply** with polished materials that speak directly to what the role needs
4. **Learn** from feedback and track what works — then use those insights to improve the next application

This all runs through Claude Code, meaning you can automate the entire pipeline or use it step-by-step.

## What We Need to Build First

Before ApplyFlow can do any of this, it needs to know *you* — deeply and specifically.

This onboarding session builds your **career context knowledge base**. This is the foundation that makes everything personalized:

- Your actual work history (not job descriptions — what you really did and built)
- Your genuine interests and criteria (not what sounds good, but what actually energizes you)
- Your writing voice (so cover letters sound like you)
- Your accomplishments with numbers (so we can tailor to each opportunity)
- Your skills and positioning (so we match to roles strategically)

We will go through a structured interview in **7 phases**, taking roughly 45-90 minutes depending on your depth. After each phase, you can review and correct.

If you prefer to skip the interview, you can instead **upload existing documents** (resume, LinkedIn profile, cover letters you have written) and we will extract and organize the information automatically.

## Phase 1: Identity & Contact

Let us start with the basics.

**Please provide:**

1. Full name

Once you provide this, I will save it to your profile and we will move forward.

---

## Onboarding Interview Flow

Once Phase 1 is complete, we will move through the remaining phases:

- **Phase 2: Career History** — For each job, what you actually did (day to day), accomplishments with numbers, what you learned
- **Phase 3: Education** — Degrees, schools, certifications, relevant coursework
- **Phase 4: Skills & Tools** — Technical tools, languages, frameworks, methodologies
- **Phase 5: Job Search Criteria** — Target roles, industries, company stage, geography, compensation, work environment preferences
- **Phase 6: Writing Voice** — Your authentic writing style (we will analyze examples you provide)
- **Phase 7: Resume Bullets** — Extract pre-approved accomplishment statements from your work history

After Phase 7, I will generate a summary of everything created and confirm we are ready to move forward.
